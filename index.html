Meet the Doctor

"Patients who started with me in 2005 are still here. Their kids are patients now. That's the practice I wanted to build."

Dr. Kimberly M. Lim, DDS opened Acworth Family Dentistry in 2005. Twenty years later, the same dentist is still seeing patients at the same location — 3104 Creekside Village Drive. Adults, children, families across three generations.

CareCredit financing accepted. Most major dental insurance plans accepted. The practice serves Kennesaw, Acworth, Marietta, Woodstock, and Dallas — patients who often started here because a neighbor recommended us, and stayed because they never had to think about it again.
